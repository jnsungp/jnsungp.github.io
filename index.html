Research

My research aims to build robust and generalizable robot autonomy by leveraging multimodal sensing (RGB, depth, contact-force), foundation robot policies, and cross-embodiment learning. I focus on Learning-Based Control, robotic manipulation, and scalable policy adaptation across different robot morphologies.

Unitree G1 Pick-and-Place Multimodal Dataset
Junsung Park
Multimodal Robot Manipulation Dataset
Dataset / HuggingFace Profile

A large-scale multimodal manipulation dataset built using a customized MuJoCo + RoboCasa + cuRobo pipeline. Includes synchronized RGB, depth, binary contact-state, 8D force–torque signals, proprioception, and motion-planned trajectories for training and evaluating foundation robot policies on the Unitree G1 humanoid.
