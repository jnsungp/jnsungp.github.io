Research

My research focuses on robot learning, cross-embodiment policy transfer, multimodal representation learning, and offline RL. I aim to build robust manipulation policies that leverage vision, depth, and contact-force signals to generalize across robotic embodiments.

A policy-free offline IRL framework that jointly optimizes rewards and conservative Q-functions via bi-level optimization, improving robustness and expert-likeness under dataset shift.
